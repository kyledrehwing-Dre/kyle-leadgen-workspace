**Problem:** ZoomInfo's default confidence score filter (75-99%) was filtering out valid contacts, causing false "Not Found" results.

**Lesson learned:**
- ZoomInfo shows "No Results Found" even when contact exists, because their confidence score is below the threshold
- Always check the confidence score dropdown in top-right corner BEFORE declaring "Not Found"
- Change from "75-99%" to "All contacts" (50-100%) when contact appears missing
